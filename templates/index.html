Tips for Best Results

Get the most out of your animations

Clear Body Shape

Drawings with visible head, body, arms, and legs work best. Stick figures are perfect!

Front-Facing Pose

Characters facing forward or at slight angles give the AI the best data for motion mapping.

Simple Background

White or simple backgrounds help the AI focus on your character without distractions.

Good Contrast

Dark lines on light paper. The more contrast between character and background, the better.
